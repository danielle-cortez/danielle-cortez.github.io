About Me

A storyteller
with a lens

I'm Danielle — a London-based photographer drawn to the world through a digital lens. It started simply: a desire to travel, to explore unfamiliar places, and to hold onto the feeling of being somewhere new. Photography became the way I do that.

My work is moody, cinematic, and atmospheric — rooted in documentary instinct and shaped by the streets, interiors, and quiet corners I've moved through. I shoot what I find, the way I find it, and I want anyone looking at the image to feel like they were standing right there.

Now based in London with 3–5 years behind the camera, I work with clients on portraits, events, travel commissions, and brand projects — bringing that same immersive, honest eye to every shoot.
